# OUTFRONT Media (outfront-media)
OUTFRONT Media is a U.S. out-of-home (OOH) advertising company and real estate investment trust (NYSE: OUT) operating a national portfolio of billboards, digital displays, and transit advertising assets. The company runs the MTA Advertising Network across New York City's subways, buses, and commuter rail, along with roadside billboards, digital liveboards, Times Square spectaculars, and place-based displays in major U.S. markets. OUTFRONT's digital inventory is made available programmatically through DOOH supply-side partners such as Vistar Media and Place Exchange. OUTFRONT sold its Canadian operations to Bell Media in October 2022 to focus on the U.S. market. OUTFRONT does not publish a developer portal or public APIs; programmatic access to its inventory is mediated through third-party DOOH SSPs.

## APIs

No public APIs have been documented. OUTFRONT Media does not operate a developer portal or expose direct REST APIs to advertisers. Its digital out-of-home inventory is made available programmatically through third-party DOOH supply-side platforms (Vistar Media, Place Exchange), which provide the API and OpenRTB-based access used by demand-side platforms and agencies.
